marine food stalls:

This is an application to facilitate a job in a food stall, 
especially for the cashier, this food stall provides a number of 30 separate dining tables 
with the number of each table and several menus to be ordered, before going to the dining 
table consumers must order food at the cashier more first, then it will be directed to choose 
the available table with the amount of seat capacity, if the table chosen by the consumer is 
available but the consumer capacity exceeds the seat capacity this cannot be done and this 
will be confirmed by the cashier. The consumer will pay for the order after the customer completes his meal, 
and the table is available again.
